CityLocation Property

Description

The CityLocation dataproperty lets you choose a city from a series of dropdowns. It consists of 3 dropdown fields: country, region and city. Depending on the country chosen the region dropdown may or may not be available.

The cities available are those used in weather widgets such as the Xaraya weather module. In other words, they correspond to weather station locations.

This property has no configuration properties.

Usage

Standalone

    <xar:data-input type="citylocation" />
    <xar:data-output type="citylocation" />

Bound

    <xar:data-input property="$properties['citylocation']" />
    <xar:data-output property="$properties['citylocation']" />
